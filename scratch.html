Football Has Always Been Part of My Life

Seahawks fan. Duck for life. Former QB, RB & LB.

How It All Started

My dad taught me how to play football when I was just a kid, and the game has been part of my life ever since. I played all the way through high school, lining up at quarterback, running back, and linebacker depending on what my team needed that season.

Even though I don't suit up anymore, I still watch as much football as I can every fall, and I'm always the loudest person in the room during a close fourth quarter.

Who I Root For

Seattle Seahawks

My favorite NFL team. There's nothing quite like the energy of the 12s, and I've followed this team through the highs and lows for years.

Oregon Ducks

Honestly, I love college football even more than the NFL, and the Ducks are my team above everyone else. Autzen Stadium on a night game is one of the best atmospheres in sports.

Oregon Ducks Quarterbacks Now in the NFL

Oregon has become one of the best programs in the country for producing NFL quarterbacks. Here are some of the most well-known names who played for the Ducks before making it to the league:
